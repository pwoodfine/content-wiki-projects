## Featured article

**[[co-location-ranking-system|The Retail Co-location Ranking System]]**

The Woodfine co-location ranking system evaluates retail sites using a deterministic algorithm that scores each hypermarket anchor location based on the convergence of secondary and tertiary retail and civic categories within defined catchment radii.

The system produces a 12-rank index mapped to five quality tiers, visualised on the GIS platform using a warm-to-cool colour scale. At the apex is the **Tier 5 (Full Co-location)**, representing sites where an anchor operates within 3 km of both a hardware superstore and a warehouse club, and within 5 km of both a healthcare facility and a university. This indicates that all four independent capital-selection processes have converged on the exact same geographic node.

## Did you know...

- **Global scale:** The current dataset identifies **2,738 ranked co-location sites** globally, encompassing 2,488 in North America and 250 in Europe.
- **Apex scarcity:** As of May 2026, there are only **102 Tier 5 (Full Co-location)** sites across North America. Rank-1 sites represent just 3.7% of total anchors, strictly enforcing index scarcity.
- **Data provenance:** The platform analyzes over 31,000 individual retail locations across 60+ chains, fusing OpenStreetMap service-business data with Overture Maps Foundation civic infrastructure records.
